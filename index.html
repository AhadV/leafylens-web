Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about LeafyLens

App Features
Technical
Account & Setup
How accurate is the plant identification?
LeafyLens identifies plants with over 95% accuracy for our database of 380,000+ plant species. Our AI has been trained on millions of plant images in various conditions (different lighting, angles, growth stages) to ensure reliable results even with partial plant photos. The more of the plant you can capture in your photo, the more accurate the identification will be.
How do the water reminders work?
After adding a plant to your collection, LeafyLens automatically suggests a watering schedule based on the plant species, season, and your local climate. You can customize this schedule if needed, and the app will send you timely notifications when it's time to water each plant. The system adapts over time based on your feedback about how your plants are doing!
Can LeafyLens diagnose plant diseases?
Yes! Our Plant ER feature can identify over 50 common plant diseases and nutrient deficiencies. Simply take a photo of the affected area, and LeafyLens will provide a diagnosis along with a treatment plan to nurse your plant back to health. For complex cases, you can also connect with plant experts through our community for personalized advice.
How does the plant sitter feature work?
The Plant Sitter feature generates a comprehensive care guide for all your plants that you can share with anyone watching your plants while you're away. It includes detailed watering schedules, light requirements, and special care instructions customized to each plant. You can share this via email, text, or a shareable link, and your sitter can even document care with photos that sync to your account.
Is LeafyLens available for Android devices?
Currently, LeafyLens is only available for iOS devices. We're working hard on an Android version that we plan to release later this year. Sign up for our newsletter to be notified when it launches! If you'd like to be considered for our Android beta testing program, you can register your interest through our website.
Does LeafyLens work offline?
Most basic features of LeafyLens require an internet connection, especially the plant identification feature which uses our cloud-based AI system. However, you can access your plant collection, view care instructions, and check watering schedules in offline mode. Any changes or new photos you take while offline will sync once you're connected again.
How often is the plant database updated?
Our plant database is continuously expanding and is updated monthly with new species, care information, and identification improvements. Our team of botanists and plant specialists works with global partners to ensure our information is accurate and comprehensive. We also incorporate user feedback to improve care recommendations based on real-world results.
Is my plant data private and secure?
Yes, your plant data is private and secure. We use industry-standard encryption and security practices to protect your information. Photos you share in the community are visible to other users, but your personal collection remains private unless you specifically choose to share it. You can manage privacy settings for individual plants and control what information is shared.
Do I need to create an account to use LeafyLens?
While you can use the basic plant identification feature without an account, creating a free account unlocks all features including plant tracking, care reminders, community access, and the ability to save your identified plants to your collection. Creating an account also ensures your plant data is backed up and accessible across multiple devices.
Can I set up multiple plant locations or homes?
Yes, LeafyLens allows you to organize your plants by location. You can create multiple locations such as "Home," "Office," "Patio," etc., making it easy to manage plants in different environments. Each location can have its own light conditions and climate settings, which helps LeafyLens provide more accurate care recommendations for plants in those specific environments.
Can I transfer my plants to a new phone?
Yes, when you set up LeafyLens on a new device, simply log in with your account credentials and all your plant data will automatically sync to your new device. This includes your plant collection, care history, photos, notes, and settings. Your data is securely stored in the cloud, making switching devices seamless.
Can multiple people manage the same plant collection?
Yes, with our Family Share feature, you can invite up to 5 household members to manage the same plant collection. Each person can access the shared plants, receive care reminders, and record care activities. This is perfect for households where plant care responsibilities are shared among family members or roommates.
