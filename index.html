Intelligence rooted in nature

Instant Plant Recognition

Take a photo of any plant, and LeafyLens will instantly identify it, providing comprehensive care information tailored to your specific environment.

Health Detective

Notice yellow leaves or wilting stems? Our diagnostic technology pinpoints exactly what's wrong and shows you precisely how to fix it with visual guides highlighting problem areas.

Smart Location Matching

Snap photos of your home's empty spots, and our app analyzes light levels, humidity, and spatial dimensions to recommend the perfect plants for each location.
