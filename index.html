Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about LeafyLens.

How accurate is LeafyLens at identifying plants?

LeafyLens boasts a remarkable 99.2% accuracy rate for plant identification, making it the most reliable plant ID app available. Our proprietary AI has been trained on millions of plant images across all growth stages and viewing angles. For best results, take clear photos in good lighting, and try to include distinctive features like flowers, leaves, or bark patterns.

Do I need to create an account to use LeafyLens?

You can use LeafyLens's basic functions without creating an account, but to save your identified plants, track your collection, and access personalized care reminders, you'll need to sign up. Creating an account is free and takes just a few seconds. For premium features, you'll need to subscribe to one of our paid plans.

Can LeafyLens identify sick or diseased plants?

Yes! Our plant diagnostic feature can identify over 200 common plant diseases, nutrient deficiencies, and pest infestations. Simply take a clear photo of the affected area, and LeafyLens will analyze the symptoms and provide a diagnosis along with a customized treatment plan. This feature is available to Premium and Expert subscribers.

How do I cancel my subscription?

You can cancel your subscription at any time by visiting the Manage Subscription page in your account settings or by contacting our support team. If you cancel during your free trial period, you won't be charged. If you cancel after being billed, you'll continue to have access to premium features until the end of your current billing cycle.

Does LeafyLens work offline?

The free version of LeafyLens requires an internet connection for plant identification. Expert subscribers can download our offline database to identify common plants without an internet connection—perfect for hiking, travel, or areas with poor reception. Note that some advanced features still require connectivity.

Who are the experts behind LeafyLens's consultation feature?

Our expert consultation team consists of professional botanists, horticulturists, and plant pathologists from around the world. All consultants have advanced degrees in their fields and extensive practical experience. They're available to answer your questions about plant identification, care, disease treatment, and propagation techniques.
